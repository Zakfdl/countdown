# Business Plan: $500–$2000/month

## Pricing Strategy
| Tier | Price | Target user | Limits |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Basic | $9/mo | New stores | 3 campaigns, basic layouts |
| Pro | $19/mo | Growing stores | 15 campaigns, templates, targeting |
| Advanced | $39/mo | Scale merchants | Unlimited campaigns, priority support, advanced analytics |

## Revenue Scenarios
- **$500/mo**: 20 Pro + 13 Basic
- **$1000/mo**: 35 Pro + 15 Basic + 5 Advanced
- **$2000/mo**: 55 Pro + 25 Basic + 15 Advanced

## Go-to-Market (Solo Founder)
1. Publish Arabic-first educational content: “How to run White Friday countdown campaigns in Salla”.
2. Share before/after conversion case studies.
3. Offer free migration/setup for first 20 merchants.
4. Partner with Salla freelancers/agencies.

## Acquisition Channels
- X/Twitter (Arabic e-commerce creators)
- YouTube short tutorials
- Salla community groups
- Direct outreach to stores with seasonal campaigns

## Outreach Plan
- 20 personalized DMs/week to Salla merchants.
- 5 agency outreach messages/week.
- 1 new case study/week.

## Launch Offers
- 14-day Pro trial
- “Founding merchants” discount 30% for 6 months
- Free template pack (Ramadan/Eid/White Friday)

## Retention Strategy
- In-app “campaign health” checklist
- Auto recommendations (best layout per page)
- Monthly seasonal templates
- Win-back email with 1-click campaign restore

## Upsell Ideas
- AI copy suggestions for urgency text
- Revenue attribution dashboard
- Geo-based countdown personalization

## Competitor Positioning Angle
“Built for Salla first: better Arabic UX, seasonal local templates, and faster setup than generic countdown apps.”

## 30-Day Action Plan
- Week 1: release MVP app listing + onboarding guide.
- Week 2: onboard first 10 merchants manually.
- Week 3: collect testimonials and improve templates.
- Week 4: launch referral incentive for agencies.

## 90-Day Growth Plan
- Month 1: reach 20 paying merchants.
- Month 2: ship analytics dashboard and reduce churn.
- Month 3: reach 60+ paying merchants and expand partner channel.
