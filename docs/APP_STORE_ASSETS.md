## Short Description
Create high-converting countdown campaigns in minutes: top bars, product timers, homepage banners, and bilingual urgency messaging.

## Long Description
Countdown Growth is a conversion-focused marketing app for Salla merchants. Build fixed-date or evergreen countdown campaigns, target specific pages or products, and localize every message in Arabic and English. Use premium templates (Ramadan, Eid, White Friday, product launch, clearance), customize design instantly, and track performance through analytics webhooks.

## Feature Bullets
- Sticky top bar and product-page countdown layouts
- Fixed-date, evergreen, and recurring timers
- CTA buttons and urgency messaging
- Arabic + English localized campaigns
- Page/product/collection targeting rules
- Expiry behaviors: hide, message, or auto-reset
- Campaign templates for major seasonal events
- Analytics hook support for event tracking
