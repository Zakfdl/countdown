# Legal Drafts

## Support Policy (Draft)
- Support channels: email + in-app form.
- Business hours: Sun-Thu, 9:00-18:00 Arabia Standard Time.
- SLA: first response within 24 business hours on paid plans.
- Incident updates shared via status page/email.

## Privacy Policy (Draft)
We collect merchant account identifiers, app settings, and campaign configuration needed to deliver countdown functionality. OAuth tokens are stored securely and used only for authorized Salla API interactions. We do not sell merchant data. Analytics webhook events are sent only if configured by the merchant. Merchants can request deletion of their data at any time.

## Terms of Service (Draft)
The app is provided as a subscription SaaS service. Merchants are responsible for campaign content accuracy and legal compliance in their jurisdiction. Misuse, abuse, or attempts to compromise service integrity may result in suspension. Liability is limited to fees paid in the previous billing period where permitted by law.

## Merchant FAQ
**Q: Can I run multiple campaigns at once?** Yes, campaigns are filtered by display rules and page context.

**Q: Does it support Arabic and English?** Yes, each campaign has bilingual text fields.

**Q: What if the timer expires?** Choose hide, show expiry message, or auto-reset.

**Q: Can I target specific products?** Yes, with product ID / collection ID targeting.
